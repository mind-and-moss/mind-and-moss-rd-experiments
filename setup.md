## Flags & cross-references (read before locking specs)

These are the items where the inventory **touches an open decision in `decisions-pending.md`**.

1. **UV resin in inventory ≠ NOA optical adhesive.** The hard-type UV resin Isaiah owns is for casting/jewelry. If decision #1 (Gem seam type) lands on UV optical bonding, **NOA 65** (or NOA 148 for borosilicate) is a separate purchase.
2. **1 mm Egofine acrylic is NOT wall material.** Confirms decision #12 edit. Use for prototyping/jigs/light-pipe tests/mockups; structural needs 3 mm+ cast acrylic or the 6 mm aquarium glass.
3. **Mini table saw cannot cut glass.** RTOVZON 96W is rated Wood/PCB/Acrylic/Plastic. Glass = score-and-snap with the glass cutter, then grind.
4. **Aquarium silicone brand unverified.** "Big Rizy Gard" doesn't match a known brand — confirm label says 100% silicone, aquarium-safe, no mildewcide before any tank seam.
5. **Two Fluval substrates serve different purposes.** Bio Stratum and Stratum aren't interchangeable. Stratum is acidifying / shrimp-focused. Plan placement accordingly.
6. **Organic potting mix needs Walstad-vetting.** Confirm no synthetic fertilizer, no wetting agents, ideally no perlite before any soil-based aquarium build (decision #8).
7. **Calcium carbonate substrate ≠ Walstad-compatible.** Buffers pH 7.5–8.5+. Earmark for hardwater/snail/shrimp builds, not planted.
8. **Goldfish are incompatible with planted Walstad builds.** Decision #8 implication. Goldfish food + meds in inventory means there's a goldfish-keeping context separate from the flagship builds.
9. **Sandpaper ceiling at 600 grit cloth.** Adequate for general work. High-finish epoxy / Gem polish needs 800 → 1500 → 2000+ wet-dry plus polishing compound. **Added to "buy" status above.**
10. **Springtails + snails + established mosses = live-culture asset.** Sealed-SKU side of decision #9 has seed stock ready.
11. **Right-angle prism is on hand for Gem centerpiece experiments** (decision #10 — suspended vs structural). Light-scatter mockups can start without further purchases.
