# References — The Machine (imitation examples)

Real built work, named builders, and commercial / research references that resemble pieces of what The Machine is targeting. Cross-references `products/the-machine/` for the AI-excavation content. See `findings/references/README.md` for tag conventions.

## Multi-tank racetrack / loop aquariums

- [imitation-example] **The King of DIY (Joey Mullen) — Triple aquarium fish bridge** — Build connecting three tanks with two clear acrylic bridges so clown loaches swim a continuous circuit between tanks. Closest hobby-scale version of Isaiah's racetrack idea. URL: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=seBch2HWlZs
- [imitation-example] **Infinity Aquarium Design — 1200-gallon Race Track Reef** — Commercial racetrack-shaped reef tank (Las Vegas firm) where fish swim a continuous loop around a central island. Aesthetically a faux reef, but the geometry is exactly the racetrack. URL: https://infinityaquarium.com/1200-gallon-race-track-with-faux-reef/
- [imitation-example] **ZIRC (Zebrafish International Resource Center) recirculating rack** — Two-faced rack system with 2×49 3.6 L tanks all plumbed into one shared recirculating water system. Engineering reference for zebrafish-specific multi-tank flow (aesthetics are wrong but the engineering is the most relevant precedent). URL: https://www.researchgate.net/figure/ZIRC-racks-and-tanks-ZIRC-uses-two-faced-rack-systems-with-2x49-36-L-tanks-A-Side_fig2_304522801

## Segmented / circular acrylic tank construction

- [imitation-example] **Brad Newton — "Building an Acrylic Aquarium" (Professional Plastics PDF)** — Long-form documented build covering cell-cast acrylic, edge prep (120 to 400 grit), and Weld-On solvent welding of flat panels. The reference text most DIY tank builders cite. URL: https://www.professionalplastics.com/professionalplastics/BuildingAnAquarium2.pdf
- [imitation-example] **Instructables — "Building an Hexagonal Acrylic Aquarium"** — Flat-panel polygonal tank (hexagon) bonded with acrylic cement; the closest published DIY analog to a segmented circular tank made of flat facets. URL: https://www.instructables.com/Building-an-Hexagonal-Acrylic-Aquarium/

> Gap noted by the research pass: no strong matches for true PVC-connector segmented circular builds. This is genuinely uncharted DIY territory — possible Mind and Moss differentiator.

## Industrial-aesthetic / brutalist aquariums

- [imitation-example] **Tatiana Bilbao Estudio — Sea of Cortez Research Center, Mazatlán** — Monumental rose-tinted raw-concrete aquarium building with cenote-shaped light wells; "flooded ruin" concept. Reigning brutalist-meets-living-water reference. URL: https://www.archpaper.com/2024/04/tatiana-bilbao-estudio-designs-an-aquarium-in-mazatlan-mexico-around-the-premise-of-a-flooded-ruin/
- [imitation-example] **Todd's 2500-gallon basement cement stingray tank (MonsterFishKeepers, 2005)** — 17 ft × 6.5 ft × 4.5 ft poured-concrete tank, 6"-thick rebar-reinforced walls, exposed sump plumbing, dual acrylic windows. Documented build journal — the literal "concrete machine" hobby reference. URL: https://www.monsterfishkeepers.com/forums/threads/todd-builds-his-2500-gallon-cement-aquarium-for-stingrays-in-the-basement.23529/
- [imitation-example] **New England Aquarium (1969, Cambridge Seven Associates)** — Original brutalist exposed-concrete interior wraps the central Giant Ocean Tank; living tank set inside raw concrete architecture.

## O2 saturator / oxygen reactor builds

- [imitation-example] **Freedom Fish Farms — DIY Speece cone install (Facebook, 2020)** — Working farm fabricated and installed a Speece cone with a venturi pre-injector, posted construction photos and tuning notes. Real built unit by named operation. URL: https://www.facebook.com/FreedomFishFarms/posts/674960033255685
- **Dr. Richard Speece (Vanderbilt) — Speece cone background (Global Seafood Alliance)** — Inventor reference for the down-flow bubble contactor; write-up shows real installations and operating parameters (25-50 mg/L DO, ≤21 psi limits). Engineering source, not pure imitation. URL: https://www.globalseafood.org/advocate/speece-cones-saturate-water-with-oxygen-ozone/
- **Robert Strange (UTK WFS 556) — DIY three-pipe stepped saturator** — Course materials describe the homebrew approximation: three PVC pipes of increasing diameter union-fitted to mimic a cone's velocity gradient. Clear DIY recipe. URL: http://web.utk.edu/~rstrange/wfs556/html-content/18-ox-cont.html

## Long-form / cascade aquarium installations

> Initially flagged as whitespace, but a second-pass research found a strong direct match: Casco Pet's Cascade Plant Display. Whitespace was real for hobbyist DIY cascades — the commercial precedent below is the reference.

- [imitation-example] **Casco Pet — Cascade Plant Display** — Stacked, water-shared cascading aquariums with sump filtration and 5000 L/h pump; built for retail aquatic-plant display. Water flows top-down through stacked tanks as one integrated system. **Direct precedent for The Machine's tank-to-tank cascade.** URL: https://cascopet.com/us/products-and-services/aquatic/aquatic-plant-displays/cascade-aquarium/
- [imitation-example] **Casco Pet — Cascading Wave Aquarium** — Companion product, same hydraulic principle. URL: https://cascopet.com/us/products-and-services/aquatic/aquatic-plant-displays/cascading-wave-aquarium/
- [imitation-example] **Aquatic Design Centre — Damien Hirst "Lost Love" (Milan, 2000)** — Large bespoke art-installation aquarium. Reference for treating an aquarium as a single-object art installation. URL: https://www.aquaticdesign.co.uk/damien-hirst-lost-love-2000-aquarium-installation-milan-italy/
- [imitation-example] **Reef2Reef — "Gravity-feeding frag tank from main display"** — Hobbyist precedent for tank-to-tank gravity overflow with cautions about overflow risk. URL: https://www.reef2reef.com/threads/gravity-feeding-frag-tank-from-main-display.904264/
- **Vertical aquaponics tower stacks** — Same hydraulic principle (water pumped up, gravity-cascades down through planted columns, returns to fish tank). Adjacent industry, useful for hydraulic design. URL: https://gogreenaquaponics.com/blogs/news/vertical-aquaponics-system
