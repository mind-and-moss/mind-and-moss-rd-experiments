# References — Production-Scale Terrarium / Vivarium Racks

External sources and imitation examples for consolidating many mini-terraria into a single rack system at production scale. Cross-references `topics/ecosystem/humidity-airflow.md` (lives on `main`, will be on this branch after merge) and `topics/ecosystem/isopods.md`. See `findings/references/README.md` for tag conventions.

## Production-scale rack systems

- [imitation-example] **Isopod Factory (YouTube channel)** — Channel dedicated to a production-scale isopod breeding operation. Video content shows shelved deli-cup production at scale. Direct reference for what a Mind and Moss cleanup-crew supply line could look like. URL: https://www.youtube.com/@IsopodFactory
- [imitation-example] **YouTube — "Breeding 1000s of Isopods in DIY Terrarium"** — Build-log of a high-density isopod operation with documented rack/cup setup and profit math. URL: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kckOHCDCH64
- [imitation-example] **Dendroboard — "Rack System" thread** — Forum discussion documenting wire-rack vivarium consolidations for dart-frog breeders (multi-tank shared misting, wire shelving for heat dissipation from lights). URL: https://www.dendroboard.com/threads/rack-system.350440/
