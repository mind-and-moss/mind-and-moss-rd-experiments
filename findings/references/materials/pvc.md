# References — PVC

External sources and imitation examples for PVC sheet / pipe used as structural framing or panels in DIY aquariums. Cross-references `topics/materials/pvc.md` (lives on `main`, will be on this branch after merge) and `products/the-machine/structure.md`. See `findings/references/README.md` for tag conventions.

## PVC-framed and PVC-paneled DIY aquariums

- [imitation-example] **MonsterFishKeepers — "PVC sheet for lining a tank"** — Documented build using PVC sheet bonded as tank interior with epoxy fillets at seams. Real built piece, not just discussion. URL: https://www.monsterfishkeepers.com/forums/threads/pvc-sheet-for-lining-a-tank.607900/
- [imitation-example] **MonsterFishKeepers — "PVC bottom acrylic tank project"** — Build thread documenting an acrylic tank with a PVC bottom panel; Weld-On 40 used to chemically bond PVC to acrylic. Direct reference for the PVC-connector pattern in The Machine's structure. URL: https://www.monsterfishkeepers.com/forums/threads/pvc-bottom-acrylic-tank-project.564689/
- [imitation-example] **MonsterFishKeepers — "Aquarium made of PVC board?" (800-gal polypropylene tank)** — Discussion of an 800-gal polypropylene-sheet tank (heat-welded inside and out, encased in metal cage). Closest documented analog to a plastic-sheet-frame welded build at scale. URL: https://www.monsterfishkeepers.com/forums/threads/aquarium-made-of-pvc-board.746869/
