## Concrete-shell + acrylic/glass-window aquariums (the principle)

These are the hobbyist analogues to public-aquarium engineering — concrete as pressure vessel, acrylic/glass as viewport.

- [imitation-example] **Reef2Reef — "21-foot concrete aquarium" (whole-house build)** — Hobbyist designed a 21' × 13.5' concrete reef tank with the house built around it; viewing window installed by a fabricator. Multi-page build log with concrete-pour and window-installation photos. URL: https://www.reef2reef.com/threads/my-home-21-feet-long-concrete-aquarium.776014/
- [imitation-example] **Reef2Reef — "Anderson Family Reef" 5,000-gal concrete DIY** — Dave Anderson's documented 5K-gallon poured-concrete reef build with acrylic viewports, sealed with Pond Armor. Among the most thoroughly documented hobbyist concrete aquariums online. URL: https://www.reef2reef.com/threads/diy-concrete-aquarium-5-000-gallon-anderson-family-reef.555497/
- [imitation-example] **Reef2Reef — "Need help with Concrete-glass connection"** — Build-thread discussion specifically on the principle of concrete shell + glass/acrylic viewport, with multiple referenced builds using Pond Armor as the structural-to-viewport bridge. URL: https://www.reef2reef.com/threads/need-help-with-concrete-glass-connection.687643/
- [imitation-example] **MonsterFishKeepers — "1000+ gal concrete aquarium final concerns"** — Separate hobbyist concrete-tank build thread with structural-engineering Q&A and curing/sealing details. URL: https://www.monsterfishkeepers.com/forums/threads/1000-gal-concrete-aquarium-final-concerns.374930/

> Already cataloged in `references/products/the-machine.md` — Todd's 2500-gallon basement cement stingray tank (MonsterFishKeepers). Pair these four with that one for a comprehensive concrete-aquarium reference set.

## Sealants / coatings used in real concrete-aquarium builds

- **Pond Armor / Pond Shield epoxy** — The single most-cited sealant across documented concrete aquarium and water-feature builds. Fish-safe once cured, applied in multiple coats over cured concrete. URL: https://www.pondarmor.com/
