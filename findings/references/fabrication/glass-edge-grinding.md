## Custom-built glass-edge grinders (the Mind and Moss-specific niche)

> **Open whitespace.** No verified DIY *custom-built* belt grinder rig dedicated to aquarium glass surfaced. Most hobbyists buy a Covington wet belt sander or repurpose a 1" wet belt sander from another industry. Possible Mind and Moss differentiator: build a purpose-made edge jig with a precision fence, tooled like a luthier's binding jig.
