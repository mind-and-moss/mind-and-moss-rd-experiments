## Planter sealing — products and conversions

> Most search results were generic listicles without specific built pieces. The Pond Shield epoxy + EPDM-liner pattern is the dominant technique to imitate, but no signature maker owns this aesthetic — another whitespace area.

- [imitation-example] **Pond Armor / Pond Shield epoxy** — The brand of choice across documented planter-to-water-feature conversions; 1.5-quart kit covers ~60 sq ft, fish-safe once cured. Their site shows specs and example use cases for converting non-aquatic vessels into water-holding vessels. URL: https://www.pondarmor.com/
- [imitation-example] **OC Pond — "Convert a Planter into a Fountain (DIY Guide)"** — Walks through real conversions of standard planters into water-holding vessels using EPDM liner and epoxy, with photos of completed builds and product specs. URL: https://www.ocpond.org/can-you-convert-a-planter-into-a-fountain-essential-steps-and-considerations/
