# Sealed Planter Box — Concept

> **Pitch:** A standard planter box sealed with 2-part resin (multi-coat + silicone edges) so it can hold standing water or wet substrate without leaking. Sale-ready hybrid planter / mini-vivarium container.

## Why it might matter
- Standard ceramic and plastic planters fail when used for water-loving / semi-aquatic plants or hybrid terrarium setups.
- A reliably-sealed planter opens up product lines that need waterproof containment but don't need full aquarium glass.
- Cheaper input cost than custom acrylic / glass build.

## What's known so far

### Sealing strategy
- Multi-coat 2-part resin application
- Sanding between coats
- Silicone for edges (corners are the weak point on a square planter)
- Pondliner discussed as an alternative approach

### Sale-readiness considerations explored
- Disclosure requirements for selling sealed containers
- Warranty implications
