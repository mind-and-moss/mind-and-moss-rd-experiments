Source: RESEARCH-005 Thread 23 — "Aquarium Design Business Names" (chat #120)

## The chosen name
**Mind and Moss** — none of the brainstormed alternatives below were picked. The chosen name has stuck and matches the brand voice.

## Naming-style insight
"**Serenity Spheres**" matches The Gem's sphere/orb form. Could be a sub-brand or product-line name for The Gem family — e.g., "Mind and Moss / Serenity Spheres."

## Brand voice (carried in CLAUDE.md)
> Mind and Moss makes living ecosystems that stop people and make them ask: "how the fuck did they make that?"

Industrial brutalism + living ecosystem. Engineering precision + art-level design. Every product needs a WOW element.
